what glowy does

everything your collection deserves

🕯️
track your glow

Add pieces to your collection with a search or a photo. Every votive you've ever loved, in one warm, beautiful place. No more wondering whether you already have a specific piece during your unplanned visit to a store (we've all been there).

📷
photo identification

Point your camera at a glassybaby and let glowy identify its color family to easily add it to your collection.

✨
collect the feeling

Browse the inspiration feed to see how other collectors are displaying their pieces. Share your own windowsill moments.
